Credits

This demo stands on a stack of impressive open source work. None of the pieces were written for this project; the contribution here is gluing them together.

Retro68

Retro68 by Wolfgang Thaller is the GCC-based cross-compiler that turns modern C into 68k Mac binaries with proper resource forks. Without it, none of this exists.

BasiliskII

BasiliskII is the original 68k Macintosh emulator by Christian Bauer. The WebAssembly build used here comes from the macemu fork maintained by the Infinite Mac project.

Infinite Mac

Infinite Mac by Mihai Parparita provides the WebAssembly compile of BasiliskII, the worker glue, and the chunked-disk transport scheme that makes large disk images practical to serve from a static host.
